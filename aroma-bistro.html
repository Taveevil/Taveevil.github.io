Aroma Bistro web design

Role: designing

In this project for my web design 2 class we were tasked with taking an existing website for a restaurant and completely redesigning it. For my restaurant, I chose Aroma bistro because their food is incredible but their website doesn't do them any justice. This project is purely conceptual

Aroma Bistro is a hong kong fusion restaurant best known for its chili oil dumplings.
