About the game

Delve deep into the forest to achieve the ultimate prize! But be warned, when you use items to help you achieve your goal, it comes at the cost of your LIFE! This is our first ludum dare (or game jam in general) so we decided to go for a simple puzzle/action adventure game. BLOOD QUEST tells the story of an adorable knight who lives in a forest, and one day decides to embark on an adventure.

Controls:

  • Left Click: Attack (must have weapon)
  • Space: Dash
  • Mouse Cursor: Look Direction
  • WASD: Move
  • Scroll Wheel: Switch Weapons
