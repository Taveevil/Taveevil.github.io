Pleasant Valley

Role: Environmental design, graphics, and 3D assets

This is Site12's entry for Ludum dare 52!

The theme for this Ludum dare was "Harvest". The concept we came up was inspired by "vampire survivors" and "stardew valley". Where you fight a constant stream of enemies while also planting your enemies essentially "setting your stakes" for more reward.

Software used:

  • Clip studio EX
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe After effects
