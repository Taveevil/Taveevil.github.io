Pleasant Valley

Role: Environmental design, graphics, and 3D assets

This is Site12's entry for Ludum dare 52!

The theme for this Ludum dare was "Harvest". The concept we came up was inpiried by "vampire survivors" and "stardew valley". Where you fight a constrant stream of enemies while also planting your own enemies essentially "setting your own stakes" for more reward.
